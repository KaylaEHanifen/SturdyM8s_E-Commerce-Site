About Us

Welcome to the Pet Rock Emporium, where we offer a unique blend of satire and novelty in the form of pet rocks. This repository hosts our capstone project—an e-commerce website developed using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. In response to the growing loneliness epidemic, our e-commerce site takes a humorous approach by offering pet rocks as companions. The satire lies in the concept of a one-sided friendship, where the pet rock is there for you whenever you need a friend, without requiring any effort or care in return. Explore our repository to delve into the codebase behind this satirical e-commerce platform. From the front-end design crafted with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to the interactive elements that bring the pet rock shopping experience to life, every aspect of the project reflects our commitment to creativity and humor. Join us in combating loneliness with a touch of —welcome to the Pet Rock Emporium.
